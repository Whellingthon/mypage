About Me

I am a Vocational Training Instructor at SENAI Pindamonhangaba/SP, with extensive experience in IT, telecommunications, and technical support, having worked at companies like Connectis, Atacadão and Claro Brazil.

I hold a degree in Systems Analysis and Development from FATEC-SP, Pedagogy from UNESP, a Postgraduate degree in Mobile Application Development and IT in Education from UNIVIRTUA, and I'm currently pursuing a Master's degree in Big Data and Business Intelligence from ENEB.

My teaching philosophy revolves around fostering critical thinking and practical application of knowledge to prepare students for the challenges of the modern workplace.
